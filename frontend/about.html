About Zilha Parishad Chandrapur

Zilha Parishad Chandrapur is a district-level government body responsible for rural development and public health management. This platform provides a data-driven approach to monitor health conditions across villages.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To build a healthy, malnutrition-free and disease-free rural community.

Mission

  • Reduce SAM & MAM cases in rural areas
  • Prevent malaria through early detection
  • Strengthen healthcare delivery systems
  • Use data for smarter decision-making

Key Responsibilities

  • Village-level health monitoring
  • Organizing health camps
  • Implementing government schemes
  • Providing child nutrition support
  • Coordinating with healthcare workers

Health Focus Areas

SAM & MAM

Severe Acute Malnutrition (SAM) and Moderate Acute Malnutrition (MAM) are critical indicators of child health. Monitoring these helps identify high-risk villages.

Malaria Control

Awareness programs, medical camps, and early diagnosis are used to control malaria spread in rural areas.

Use of Technology

This dashboard uses analytics and visualization tools to provide insights into village health data, enabling faster and better decision-making.
