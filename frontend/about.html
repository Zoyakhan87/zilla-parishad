About Zilha Parishad Chandrapur

This portal visualizes health-driven SAM / MAM data for Chandrapur villages. The goal is to help decision-makers quickly identify most affected areas and plan health interventions.

Objectives

  • Provide an easy-to-use dashboard for SAM/MAM analysis
  • Enable multi-lingual accessibility (Marathi / Hindi / English)
  • Offer chatbot assistance for rapid queries
